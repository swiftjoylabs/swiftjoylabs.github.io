About HuatSays

HuatSays generates Chinese New Year greetings themed around the zodiac animal of the current year. For 2026, greetings are themed around the Year of the Horse. Simply tap to refresh and discover new greetings, or unlock additional greeting packs through in-app purchases.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a new greeting?

Tap the refresh button to generate a new Chinese New Year greeting. Each greeting is themed around the Year of the Horse for 2026.

What are greeting packs?

Greeting packs are additional collections of themed greetings that you can purchase within the app. Each pack contains unique greetings to give you more variety when sharing festive wishes.

How do I purchase additional greeting packs?

In the app, look for the option to browse available greeting packs. Tap on a pack to preview it, then confirm your purchase through the App Store. Purchases are processed securely by Apple.

I purchased a greeting pack but it's not showing up. What do I do?

Try the following steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Make sure you have an active internet connection
  3. Use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app to recover your purchases
  4. If the issue persists, email us at hello@swiftjoylabs.com

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase, then use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app. Your previously purchased greeting packs will be restored at no additional cost.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the purchase and click "Report a Problem"
  4. Select the reason and submit

The app crashed. What should I do?

Please try the following:

  1. Force quit the app and restart it
  2. Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  3. Restart your device if the issue persists
  4. Email us at hello@swiftjoylabs.com with details about what happened

Is my data private?

Yes. HuatSays stores data locally on your device. We do not collect personal information. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
