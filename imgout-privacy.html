ImgOut Privacy Policy

Image Extractor for PowerPoint & Word Files

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

This page describes privacy practices specific to ImgOut. It supplements our general Privacy Policy, which also applies to your use of this app.

1. Data Collection

ImgOut is designed to collect as little data as possible. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Documents you import into the app
  • Images extracted from your documents
  • File names, sizes, or metadata
  • Any content within your files

The only information we may collect is crash reports and device information to help diagnose and fix technical issues, and any communications you send us directly (e.g. support emails).

2. Local Processing

All document processing happens entirely on your device. When you import a file, ImgOut parses it locally and displays the extracted images within the app. No files or images are ever uploaded to our servers or any third-party server at any point during this process.

Extracted images are stored temporarily in the app's local cache. This cache is cleared when you close the document or delete the app.

3. Photos Library Access

ImgOut requests permission to access your Photos library solely to allow you to save extracted images when you explicitly choose to tap "Save to Photos." Specifically:

  • ImgOut never reads, scans, or accesses your existing photos
  • Photos permission is only used to write images you choose to save
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos

4. Third-Party Services

ImgOut may use a crash reporting service to help us identify and fix bugs. This service may collect device type and OS version, but does not have access to your documents or extracted images. No analytics services are used to track how you use the app or what files you process.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

ImgOut offers in-app subscriptions processed entirely by Apple. We do not store or handle your payment information. For subscription billing details, please see the ImgOut Terms of Service.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how ImgOut handles your data, please contact us at:

Email: support@swiftjoylabs.com
