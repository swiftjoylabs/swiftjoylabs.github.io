Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my data?

Go to the app's Settings → Data → Reset. Note that this action cannot be undone, so make sure to back up any important information first.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple App Store account:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select the SwiftJoyLabs subscription
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the purchase and click "Report a Problem"
  4. Select the reason and submit

The app crashed. What should I do?

We're sorry for the inconvenience! Please:

  1. Force quit the app and restart it
  2. Make sure you're running the latest version
  3. Restart your device if the issue persists
  4. Email us at hello@swiftjoylabs.com with details

How do I sync across devices?

If the app supports iCloud sync, make sure:

  • You're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices
  • iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud
  • The app has permission to use iCloud (check in Settings → [App Name])

Is my data private?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Most data is stored locally on your device. For details, please read our Privacy Policy.
