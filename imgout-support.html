About ImgOut

ImgOut extracts embedded images from Microsoft PowerPoint (.pptx) and Word (.docx) documents directly on your iPhone or iPad. All processing happens locally on your device — no files are ever uploaded to any server. Simply import a document, and ImgOut will find and display all embedded images in a clean gallery view, ready to save or share.

How It Works

ImgOut parses the internal structure of your document, navigates its embedded media layers, and extracts images directly on your device. Your original file is never modified.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which file types does ImgOut support?

ImgOut supports modern Office formats: PowerPoint (.pptx) and Word (.docx). Legacy binary formats (.ppt and .doc) are not supported, as they use a different file structure. If you have a legacy file, open it in Microsoft Office or Google Slides/Docs and save it as .pptx or .docx first.

What image formats can ImgOut extract?

ImgOut can extract PNG, JPEG, GIF, TIFF, and BMP images embedded in your documents. Windows metafiles (EMF/WMF) may not render correctly on iOS.

How do I import a document?

Tap the Import button on the home screen to open the iOS document picker. You can browse files from your iPhone, iCloud Drive, or any connected third-party storage provider such as Dropbox or Google Drive.

ImgOut says "No images found." What does that mean?

This means the document does not contain any embedded images, or the images are linked externally rather than embedded in the file. Try opening the document in PowerPoint or Word and verify that images are visually present. If they are, please contact us and we'll investigate.

How do I save images to my Photos library?

In the gallery view, tap the menu (•••) on any image and choose Save to Photos. ImgOut will ask for Photos library permission the first time — tap Allow to enable saving.

ImgOut is asking for Photos permission. Why?

ImgOut needs access to your Photos library only to save extracted images when you choose to. The app never reads your existing photos. You can manage this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.

Photos permission was denied. How do I fix it?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos, find ImgOut in the list, and set access to Add Photos Only or Full Access. Then try saving again.

Extraction failed. What should I do?

Please try the following:

  1. Make sure the file is a valid .pptx or .docx (not a renamed or corrupted file)
  2. Try re-downloading or re-exporting the file
  3. Force quit the app and try again
  4. If the issue persists, email us at support@swiftjoylabs.com with the file details

The app crashed. What should I do?

Please try the following:

  1. Force quit the app and restart it
  2. Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  3. Restart your device if the issue persists
  4. Email us at support@swiftjoylabs.com with details about what happened

Does ImgOut upload my files anywhere?

No. All processing is done entirely on your device. Your documents and extracted images are never sent to any server. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
