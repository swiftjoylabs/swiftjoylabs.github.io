This page describes terms specific to ImgOut. It supplements our general Terms of Service, which also applies to your use of this app.

1. Subscription Plans

ImgOut offers the following plans. A "processing" refers to one document import and extraction session.

Plan Processings Billing Price
Free 3 per month None Free
Basic 20 per month Auto-renews monthly See App Store
Pro Monthly Unlimited Auto-renews monthly See App Store
Pro Annual Unlimited Auto-renews yearly See App Store

Processing limits reset at the start of each calendar month. Unused processings do not roll over.

2. Free Trial

New Basic and Pro subscribers (Monthly and Annual) are eligible for a 7-day free trial. The following conditions apply:

  • The free trial is available to users who have not previously subscribed to the respective plan
  • No charge is made during the trial period
  • At the end of the 7-day trial, the subscription automatically converts to a paid plan
  • To avoid being charged, you must cancel at least 24 hours before the trial expires
  • You can cancel in your Apple App Store account settings: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions

3. Billing and Renewal

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple App Store account settings
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access until then
  • We may change subscription pricing with advance notice

4. Refunds

All refunds are handled by Apple according to their refund policies. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
